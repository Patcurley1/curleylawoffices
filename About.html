About Us

The Law Offices of Richard T. Curley, P.C. is a general practice law firm in Boyertown, Pennsylvania. The practice emphasizes litigation, business and estate planning, all done with an eye toward the long term , positive effects that community involvement can bring to the affairs of our clients.
We believe that the best service we can provide our clients is to listen to them, and get the best outcome that can be obtained, recognizing the family, business, and community concerns that our clients have in mind.
We know each issue that you bring to us is important enough for you to seek our assistance, and we try each and every day to justify that confidence in us.
